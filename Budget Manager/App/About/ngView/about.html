About Software
The purpose of this software is to manage the budget of an institute. This software can track every allotment along with every spending. Also, there are lots of codes we have to maintain for the budget of our institutions. It's very difficult to maintain the calculations of each of those codes manually on paper. This software makes this task super easy. Now you can search your required code from this software and find out your necessary information like Task Completed, Task on Progress, Remained Balance, and plan accordingly.
